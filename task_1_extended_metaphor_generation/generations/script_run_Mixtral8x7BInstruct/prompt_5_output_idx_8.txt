"Global fanbase intersection and mathematical analysis of Kpop music chart performance." This feature describes how the international fanbase of Kpop groups, which comes from different parts of the world, intersects, and how mathematical equations and analysis can be used to describe and predict the popularity and performance of Kpop songs on music charts. This feature demonstrates the dynamic and ever-changing nature of Kpop fandom, and the geometric and mathematical principles that can be used to define and understand its success.
