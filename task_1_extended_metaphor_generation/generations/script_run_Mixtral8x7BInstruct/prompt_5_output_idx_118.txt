"Arc Reactor"

The Arc Reactor is a core component of Iron Man's suit, which efficiently distributes and utilizes energy throughout the suit's systems, similar to a distribution network in engineering or logistics. It has a complex geometric structure that allows for multiple pathways for energy flow, increasing the overall resilience and stability of the system, demonstrating redundancy.
